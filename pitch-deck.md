# Pitch Summary

BiasLens AI is a fairness intelligence platform that detects and mitigates bias in AI systems and digital content.

It combines:

* Fairness metrics (Fairlearn, AIF360)
* AI explanations (Gemini)
* Interactive dashboards

Our goal is to make AI transparent, explainable, and fair for real-world applications.

Aligned with UN SDG 10 – Reduced Inequalities.
